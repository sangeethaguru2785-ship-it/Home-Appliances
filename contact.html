FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be in original condition with packaging. Free pickup and full refund, no questions asked.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available at checkout. Free shipping on orders over $99.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, professional installation is available for major appliances. You can add installation services during checkout for a small fee.

What warranty do your products carry?

All products come with a minimum 2-year warranty. Extended warranty plans up to 5 years are available for purchase on select items.
